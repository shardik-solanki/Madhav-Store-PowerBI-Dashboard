Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard

Overview
This project is an interactive sales dashboard created using Power BI and spreadsheet data. It visualizes key metrics such as total sales, profit, quantity sold, and average order value, providing a clear view of business performance across regions, customer segments, product categories, and payment modes.

My Role
Collected, cleaned, and prepared sales data from spreadsheets for analysis.
Built data models in Power BI, including relationships and calculated fields.
Designed visualizations (bar charts, donut charts, and trend lines) to highlight key performance indicators.
Implemented insights on top-performing states, customers, sub-categories, and payment modes.
Configured filters for quarterly and overall analysis to enable dynamic exploration of the data.

Objective
The dashboard addresses the need to monitor sales performance and profitability across different dimensions. It helps stakeholders quickly identify trends, understand high-value customers and products, and make data-driven decisions to optimize revenue and operational efficiency.
